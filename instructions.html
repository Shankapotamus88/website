How to Play Snake

  • Use the ← ↑ → ↓ arrow keys to move.
  • Eat the red blocks to grow and score points.
  • Avoid running into the walls or yourself (after the 5 s grace period).
  • Your current score appears in the bottom-left corner.
  • Hit “Play Snake” (above) or reload the page to restart.
