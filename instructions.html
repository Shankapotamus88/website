How to Play Snake

  • Use the ← ↑ → ↓ arrow keys to move the snake.
  • Eat the red squares to grow and earn points.
  • If you hit the walls or yourself (after the 5-second grace), the game is over.
  • Your score appears in the bottom-left corner.
  • Reload the page (or press “Play” above) to start a new game.
